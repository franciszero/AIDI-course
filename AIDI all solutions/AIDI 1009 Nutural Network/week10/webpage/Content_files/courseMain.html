          Week 10 - Training Neural Networks Continued & Introduction to CNNs

          Content

          • Introduction

            Introduction
            In this lecture, we will finish concepts related to training neural networks like Batch Normalization, Data Augmentation, Weight Decay, Early Stopping, Bagging and Ensemble Methods & Dropout. Finally, at the end we introduce convolution neural networks, discuss problems using FC on Images and understand different layers in CNN.

          • Learning Objectives

            Learning Objectives
            • Explain concepts of Batch Normalization & Data Augmentation 
            • Discuss Weight Decay, Early Stopping, Bagging and Ensemble Methods & Dropout 
            • Discuss problems using FC on Images 
            • Understanding Convolutions on Images & Image Filters 
            • Describe Convolution Layer & Padding 
            • Understanding Pooling Layers, CNN Prototype & Receptive field

          • Summary

            Summary
            In this week's lecture, we discussed the remaining concepts under training neural networks like Batch Normalization & Data Augmentation, discussed Weight Decay, Early Stopping, Bagging and Ensemble Methods & Dropout techniques that help improve the neural network performance.
            Next week, we will discuss what is a Convolutional Neural Network and how does it work? Then we will move on to learning about the history of CNNs and discuss some of the most popular Convolutional Networks Architectures.
